Welcome to Extra Deep!

Now with over 500.000 listeners worldwide, Extra Deep is the world’s fastest growing electronic music show. Access the latest shows here, or tune in to one of the many stations that ED is broadcasted on whereever you are in the world.

INFO

Extra Deep - for us, it's a story about us, about how we see the music and about how that changes... Show was originally created in Minsk. The first episode was available 1 March 2012. Since then, more than 100+ shows have been broadcasted. More than 200.000+ downloads in the last 6 years. The radioshow is created by Igor Lukyanets (Dave Milton) and Dmitry Kubik (Jack Diss) from Minsk.
